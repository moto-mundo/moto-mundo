Mongolsk Yurte

We do lectures on our travels all over Denmark for all kinds of groups and audiences. Since we gave our first lecture in Valby Civic Centre on the motorcycle journey through South America with Emil and Ida, we have been on stage in packed auditoriums more than a thousand times. We have entertained the blind and trhe deaf, in prisons, in Norway, Sweden and Germany, and even at the NATO headquaters and for friends in Moscow. You will find information about our range of lectures. at http://ninaoghjalte.dk/?lang=uk You are most welcome to contact us for information on lectures in or outside Denmark.
